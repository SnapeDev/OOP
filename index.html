Find the Rabbit

Your rabbit is hiding in the garden! To bring it out, explore the house, collect three carrots from different rooms, and return to the garden. A golden carrot is also available, which is worth three carrots! Once you have all three carrots, the rabbit will come out of hiding.
Commands:
Use move north, move south, move east, or move west to navigate, collect carrot to collect the carrots, and feed rabbit to feed the rabbit. Good luck!
